Tree of light

About Game

Tree of Light is a story-driven experience set in a living forest world.
It's not gonna be all explosions and car chases. Think more like... remember those fairy tale books you'd read as a kid? That vibe. You know, the kind where you don't want to rush to the end.

As Lupine and Mino travel deeper into Winland, ancient symbols begin to reveal themselves. These symbols? They're old. Like, your great-great-grandmother probably knew what they meant old. Pretty neat if you ask me.

You'll need to slow down and really pay attention - this isn't your typical rush-through game.

Main Heroes

  • Lupine

    Lupine's the kind of person who stays cool under pressure and always knows what needs to be done. Lupine leads the journey toward the Tree of Light, guided by courage rather than force.

  • Mino

    Lupine’s loyal companion. Curious, steadfast, and always alert, Mino supports the journey and brings balance to every step forward.

  • Together, they represent trust, friendship, and shared determination.

Forest magic

  • Ancient Pattern Scrolls

    Hidden within the forest are magic scrolls displaying ancient patterns. When activated, these patterns grant additional strength and influence the flow of the game.

  • Support the Journey

    Every successful combination helps Lupine and Mino move closer to the Tree of Light, so you actually feel like you're helping them out.

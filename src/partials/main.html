About Game

Tree of Light is a story-driven experience set in a living forest world.
It's not gonna be all explosions and car chases. Think more like... remember those fairy tale books you'd read as a kid? That vibe. You know, the kind where you don't want to rush to the end.

As Lupine and Mino travel deeper into Winland, ancient symbols begin to reveal themselves. These symbols? They're old. Like, your great-great-grandmother probably knew what they meant old. Pretty neat if you ask me.
